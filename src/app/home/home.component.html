Dominik Neal

Product Manager

Dominik is an experienced mobile and web product manager. In his 7 years of experience, he has launched products within E-commerce platforms such as payments, chat Bots/communication, recommendation engines using machine learning, and also has implemented converging legacy systems into modernization platforms.

In his’ time in product, he has ideated and launched experiences in North America, Europe, the United Kingdom, Asia-Pacific, and South America. This has given him a full grasp of consumer trends and behaviors in multiple regions.

If you’re looking for someone to implement one of your ideas or projects, Dominik would be a great resource for you or your company. Dominik has the experience you need for leading your team and hitting the ground running from Day 1.
